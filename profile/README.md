# East West University Telecommunications Club (EWUTC)

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of the **East West University Telecommunications Club (EWUTC)**.

Founded on **14 April 2006**, EWUTC is one of the most active student-run organizations at East West University. Established by a group of passionate students, the club has continuously worked towards fostering technological excellence, professional development, innovation, and knowledge sharing among students.

Through various academic, technical, and professional initiatives, EWUTC strives to bridge the gap between classroom learning and industry requirements, preparing future leaders, innovators, and technology professionals.

---

## Our Mission

EWUTC is dedicated to:

* Promoting knowledge and awareness of modern telecommunications and information technology.
* Encouraging innovation, entrepreneurship, and research-driven learning.
* Providing practical exposure to emerging technologies and industry practices.
* Supporting students in developing technical, professional, and leadership skills.
* Creating opportunities for collaboration between academia and industry.

## Signature Events

### National Entrepreneurship Summit (NES)

Since **2018**, EWUTC has been organizing the **National Entrepreneurship Summit**, a platform dedicated to fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, leadership, and strategic thinking among students and aspiring entrepreneurs.

### Telco Warfare

Introduced nationally in **2012** and expanded internationally in **2017**, **Telco Warfare** has become one of the flagship competitions organized by EWUTC.

The competition brings together participants from various institutions across multiple domains of information technology and programming. With participation from approximately **60 institutions** and over **2,000 participants**, Telco Warfare continues to inspire learning, competition, and technological excellence.

### National Learning Fest (NLF)

The **National Learning Fest** was launched to create meaningful learning opportunities for college and university students.

The event focuses on providing practical, hands-on experiences and industry-relevant knowledge that support both academic growth and professional readiness.
